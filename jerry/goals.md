# Goals — Stablish Solutions, Inc.

## North Star (12 months)
$30K MRR + seed funding → founders go full-time.

## Two-Layer Strategy
• *Layer 1 — Core Software* (MRR engine): recurring revenue, proves PMF
• *Layer 2 — TAP* (cash acceleration): upfront cash to fund execution. Does NOT count toward MRR.

## 2026 Targets
• *Revenue:* ~$20K MRR by EOY
• *Customers:* ~105 churches live
• *Unit Economics:* ~150 givers/church × $1.25 net rev = ~$187 MRR/church
• *Break-even:* ~50 churches (burn ~$7K/month)

## 2026 Execution Phases
• *Phase 1 — Ramp (Mar–Jun):* Refine onboarding, improve usability, validate sales motion
• *Phase 2 — Steady Growth (Jul–Dec):* Consistent church acquisition, founder-led sales + SDR support

## TAP Goals (2026)
• ~105 TAP orders × ~$2,245/order = ~$235K revenue, ~$188K gross profit
• Purpose: fund engineering + onboarding + acceleration

## 2027 Goal
• Reach $27K–$30K+ MRR
• Maintain SMB base (~$20K MRR) + add 2+ large churches (~$3,750 MRR each)

## Founder Transition Trigger
Raise ~$2M seed (target ~$10M valuation) when:
• MRR is durable and growing
• Retention validated
• Sales motion repeatable

## Key Metrics Otto Tracks
• *Revenue:* MRR growth, revenue per church
• *Growth:* Churches onboarded, sales conversion rate
• *Product:* Weekly active usage, retention
• *Efficiency:* CAC per church, burn rate

## Operating Constraints
Keep burn low. Prioritize sustainability. Avoid funding dependency. Build durable revenue.

## Definition of Success
~$30K MRR + strong retention + repeatable sales + clear path to scale + ability to raise and go full-time.
