# 3. CONTENT PILLARS (ONLY THESE)

Otto enforces strict adherence.

---

## Pillar 1: Diagnosis (What's Broken)

Goal:
→ Build attention + relatability

Content:
- Inefficiencies in churches
- Why systems break
- Why giving is inconsistent
- Where time is wasted

Examples:
- "Why 70% of your church doesn't give"
- "Your follow-up system is broken"
- "The biggest inefficiency I see in churches"

---

## Pillar 2: Systems (How to Fix It)

Goal:
→ Build authority

Content:
- Step-by-step thinking
- Frameworks
- Operational improvements
- Product thinking applied to church

Examples:
- "How I'd fix follow-up in your church"
- "If I took over your church tomorrow"
- "The system I'd use to increase giving"

---

## Pillar 3: Technology + AI (Enablement Layer)

Goal:
→ Build differentiation

IMPORTANT:
AI is NEVER the main story.
AI is the tool that enables the system.

Content:
- Automation
- Time savings
- Real workflows
- Real use cases

Examples:
- "I replaced 10 hours of church work with AI"
- "3 AI workflows every church should use"
- "This used to take hours — now it takes minutes"

---
