# Stablish — Company Context

## What We Do
Fintech platform helping churches increase recurring generosity through intelligent giving and AI-driven engagement. We solve: most people want to give but don't give consistently.

## Why We Exist
Stablish is an extension of Jared's faith, not separate from it. The mission: help people gain financial control and help the Church become generous and healthy. This isn't a donation tool — it's a vehicle for generosity, stewardship, and financial freedom. Every product decision should reflect that.

## Core Products
• *Intelligent Giving* — decision + automation layer
  • *Giving Power* — shows users what they can safely afford to give (removes guesswork, builds confidence)
  • *Dynamic AutoGive* — adaptive recurring giving that adjusts based on financial conditions (charge full, reduce, or skip — no stress)
• *Stash AI* — in-app AI assistant for pastors (conversation over dashboards)
  • Answers questions ("How is giving this month?")
  • Drives action (drafts emails, follow-ups, thank-you notes)
  • Daily briefings with giving summaries and suggested actions
  • Channels: in-app chat + SMS

## Revenue Model
Church subscriptions for the giving platform + Stash AI

## Current Stage
MVP — shipping v1.0 mobile app + dashboard. Pre-scale.

## Target Customer
Small to mid-sized churches. Pastors focused on engagement and growth.

## Key Metrics
• Recurring giving rate
• Giving frequency
• Total giving volume
• Churches onboarded
• Engagement levels (Stash AI usage)

## Strategic Position
Not a donation tool — an intelligent giving system + engagement engine + AI assistant.

## Where We're Headed
1. Expand Stash AI capabilities + launch Dynamic AutoGive
2. Financial wellness tools + giving planning
3. Long-term: national giving network, millions of users, default platform for generosity
